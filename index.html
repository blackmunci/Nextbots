1.Angry Munci

However, in the Birthday update they were brightened once again. If you look closely at her eyes, you can see the faint green and pink artifacts, which were most likely compressed.(angry munci speed is 840)

What seems to be a little static on the image, and it's eyes are surrounded with thick white outlines that were half-closed. Ears aren't visible, nor have any nose. The edges around its face are wrinkly because it was poorly edited.There were faint black outlines if you examine the image closer. Highlight within each pupil was below the lower eyelid.(happy speed is 750)

3.Trauma


Trauma features a disfigured, red face with transparent holes blotting its upper head alongside a lack of skin, with its mouth twisted into a smile. It emits red light in dark areas. If you look closely at the image, you can tell there are faint red edges around it.(Trauma speed is 550)

4.Qubert

6.Sanic(normal)
